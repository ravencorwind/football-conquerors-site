Before You Contact Us

  • Verify game files in Steam.
  • Check that your GPU driver and Windows updates are current.

Include This Info In Your Email

  • Steam username and in-game display name.
  • Issue summary and exact steps to reproduce.
  • Screenshot/video if possible.
  • PC specs (CPU, GPU, RAM, OS version).
  • Date/time of issue and your timezone.
