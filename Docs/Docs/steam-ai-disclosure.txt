Steam Store "Generative AI" disclosure text (EN)
=================================================
Football Conquerors uses generative AI tools during development for some pre-generated assets and iteration workflows, including concept visuals, selected card/store artwork drafts, music drafts, and writing/code assistance.

All AI-assisted outputs are reviewed, edited, and approved by the developer before release.

The released game does not generate player-facing content or code in real time during gameplay, and it does not require a live third-party generative AI service during play.


Turkish reference version
=========================
Football Conquerors, gelistirme surecinde bazi onceden uretilmis varliklar ve yineleme is akislari icin ureten yapay zeka araclari kullanir; buna konsept gorseller, secili kart/magaza gorseli taslaklari, muzik taslaklari ve yazi/kod yardimi dahildir.

Yapay zeka destekli ciktilarin tamami yayin oncesinde gelistirici tarafindan gozden gecirilir, duzenlenir ve onaylanir.

Yayinlanan surumde oyun, oyun sirasinda gercek zamanli olarak oyuncuya gosterilen icerik veya kod uretmez ve oynanis esnasinda canli bir ucuncu taraf ureten yapay zeka servisine baglanmaz.
