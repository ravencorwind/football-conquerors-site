Privacy Policy - Football Conquerors

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1) Who We Are

Football Conquerors is developed and published by Ravencorwind. If you have privacy-related questions, contact: ravencorwind@gmail.com.

2) Data We Process

Depending on features you use, the game may process:

  • Player profile data (display name, settings, progression, achievements, quest progress).
  • Gameplay stats (match results, goals, captures, purchases, boss kills, scores).
  • Leaderboard payload data when online leaderboard is enabled (display name, score, anti-cheat proof fields, timestamps, nonce/challenge token, sequence values).
  • Technical identifiers used for leaderboard integrity (device identifier fallback, app version, basic device model/GPU summary).
  • Support contact data you provide voluntarily by email.

3) Where Data Is Stored

  • Local save data is stored on your device.
  • Online leaderboard records are stored on configured backend services and/or Unity Gaming Services (if enabled in build configuration).
  • Steam platform data is managed by Valve under Steam's own privacy policies.

4) Why We Process Data

  • To save game progress and settings.
  • To provide leaderboard ranking and anti-cheat validation.
  • To troubleshoot issues and provide player support.

5) Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be processed by service providers required to run the game features (for example hosting, leaderboard infrastructure, Unity services, and Steam platform services).

6) Retention

  • Local data remains on your device until you uninstall or clear local saves.
  • Leaderboard data may be kept while leaderboard functionality is active and for reasonable anti-abuse/security review periods.
  • Support emails are retained as needed to handle your request.

7) Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion related to your support data or leaderboard display name records. Contact us at ravencorwind@gmail.com and include your display name and platform details.

8) Children

If you are under the age required by your local law, please use the game only with parent/guardian permission.

9) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to reduce unauthorized access and abuse. No method is 100% secure.

10) Changes

We may update this policy. Updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
